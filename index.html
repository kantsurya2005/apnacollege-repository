            -Soak them in bowl full of water until we chop the vegetables.

            -Chop Paneer in cube of you prefered size.
            
            
    
            Don't put oil until the utensil is hot.

            Don't put any vegetables until oil heats up.

            

            
            Now, we have collected all the requirements.
            
            >Put some turmeric powder and salt in paneer and mix it well.
            
            >In put them in hotoil for few minutes.
            
            >Turn them around and lightly fry those cubes from both sides.
            
            >Gently take it out.

            >Add little oil if needed.
            
            >Put cumin seeds in hot oil.
            
            >Put green chilli, bae leaves and ginger garlic paste.
            
            >Then, slightly put the chopped onions and mix well.
            
            >Stir until it turns slightly brown.
            
            >Then add the chopped  onions in it.
            
            >Asa tomato is in, add salt as per your taste.
            
            >Keep stirring so that it don't stik to the base. 
            
            >When the tomato is half cooked, add the soaked spices in it with little water.
            
            >Mix it well and cover it.
            
            >After 3-4 minutes, check if some more water is neededa and stir it.
            
            >Again after 3-4 minutes, check wheather there is oil secretion from the spices or not.
            
            >If not, then check if it's stick to the base, add little  water and stir.
            
            >If oil secreted, put the paneers in and mix gently without breaking any pieces.
        
            >Add very little water and cover the lid.
            
            >After 1-2 minutes, turn off the heat supply.
            
            >Your tasty paneer chilli is ready to be served.
